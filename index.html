What I do

Launch Alignment (VR & Platforms)

Meta Quest submissions, console TRC/VRC, and mobile store requirements. I align what leadership wants to ship with what the team can realistically deliver and what platforms actually expect.

The result: predictable launches instead of heroic last-minute saves.

LiveOps & Roadmap Alignment

When LiveOps feels chaotic, the roadmap, capacity, and goals are out of sync. I untangle priorities, clarify trade-offs, and reconnect LiveOps plans with reality, so updates for your live game land on time and with less stress.

Stability first. Then sustainable growth.

Production Systems & Decision Loops

Lightweight systems for senior teams: clear decision owners, risk maps, clean boards, and recurring partner syncs that actually surface misalignment early. Enough structure to prevent drift; not enough to slow you down.

Stakeholder & Publisher Alignment

Helping studios and publishers get back on the same page: shared language for scope, quality, and timelines, realistic milestone framing, and visibility into what has been delivered versus what’s still ahead.

Monetization & Economy Decisions

Aligning business expectations with player reality: pricing experiments, pass structures, and sinks/sources mapping that are grounded in data and respectful of your audience, instead of short-term panic moves.

Leadership & Producer Coaching

1:1 and small-group coaching for leads and producers who are stuck in the gap between “what was promised” and “what’s possible”. Practical tools for setting expectations, saying no, and protecting teams from constant crisis mode.
